About Me

I am a front-end web designer, currently studying Web and Mobile Design at Rochester Institute of Technology. I'm interested in co-op opportunities where I can apply and enhance my skills. Most of my skill are within the front-end aspect of web design but I’m keen on expanding my back-end knowledge. My experiences with graphic design are with both HTML and mockups. As part of my project, I’ve done book covers reimagined in a 2-D flat design and logos/icons for local parks.
